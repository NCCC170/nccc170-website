An overview of the role and importance of multistate research within the region, emphasizing its value in fostering collaboration, leveraging shared resources, and delivering impactful outcomes across land grant institutions. It outlines the funding structure under the Hatch Act, current federal funding considerations, and the organizational framework of agInnovation North Central. Overall, the presentation underscores the committee’s role in strengthening regional research through coordinated, accountable, and strategic collaboration.
