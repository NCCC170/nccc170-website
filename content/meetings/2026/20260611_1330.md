Our ongoing project initiated in 2024 and investigates best practices for the application of Generalized Linear Mixed Models (GLMMs), including topics in statistical modeling and software implementation. As we transition into the project's next phase, this meeting will serve as a working session to review our progress and plan our future steps. We will prioritize discussions around our publication roadmap and the immediate actionable steps required to successfully deliver our findings.
